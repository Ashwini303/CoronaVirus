Corona Virus Website

Coronaviruses cause different coronavirus diseases including
severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS).Middle East respiratory
syndrome (MERS), and coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).
Some strains of coronaviruses can also cause the common cold.

Precautions

  • Wash your hands.
  • Wear masks.
  • Social distancing.
